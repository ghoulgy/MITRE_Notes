## Hunt

Look into EDR/MDR log data that contains any:  
Unicode value: `\u202e` or `U+202e` or `%u202e`  
Hex value: `%e2%80%ae`
Decimal value: `226`,`128`,`174`

Probably some of the EDR/MDR might already encoded the data with utf-8.
